TACOS

Description

Tacos are everyone's favorite food right? Well, here I'm going to teach you how to make the perfect one. Human not included.

Ingredients

  • Tortillas
  • Ground meat
  • Onions, and spices and stuff
  • Toppings!

Directions

  1. Cook the meat. Season with onions, spices like cumin, chili powder, coriander, lime, cilantro, whatever you like!
  2. Get your toppings in a row. The french say mise en place. Wash and chop veggies. Shred or crumble cheese. Pick and tear herbs. Drain pickles. You get the drill.
  3. Warm tortillas. I prefer to steam them, if you've got a steamer, otherwise wrap them in a wet paper towel and warm in the oven. Unless you've got hard shell tortillas, in which case you're crazy.
  4. Tacos...Assemble!!!! Take your beautiful tortillas and top them with your business. Start with beans, then cabbage, meat, veggies, sauce, cheese, finish with herbs. Be creative!
  5. Put on a plate, where it won't stay for long because you're going to crush these tacos! Yum. No utensils required.
