MacN'Cheese

Description

You thought I was going to detail a nice homemade recipe here didn't you? Haha, well joke's on you sucker. Here's how to make KD.

Ingredients

  • A box of KD
  • Some Butter
  • Some Milk
  • Salt and Pepper, if you're fancy

Directions

  1. Get a pretty big pot and fill it with 6 cups water, then set it on the stove on high heat to boil. If you're fancy, season the water with a bunch of salt to make the noodles tastier.
    Not necessary though, because the cheese sauce should do the heavy lifting there. Note: The bigger the pot the better, which will stop the pasta from boiling over.
  2. Once the pot of water is boiling, open up the package of KD, carefully remove the sauce packet, and pour the noodles into the boiling water. Give em a good stir so they don't stick to
    the bottom of the pot, and a bit of butter as well. The fat from the butter will again help the pasta from boiling over. Set timer for 8 minutes.
  3. When the timer goes off, turn off the stove, drain the pasta by pouring through a sieve in the sink. Return the cooked pasta to the pot, swirling it to keep the pasta from sticking.
  4. Add a bit of butter, a splash of milk, and empty the sauce packet into the pot. Put the pot back onto the same element, which is now turned off. (The residual heat should be enough
    keep the pasta warm.) Stir to combine until the sauce is consistent and there are no clumps of powder or butter.
  5. Again, if you're fancy, season with salt and pepper and serve in a bowl. If not, eat straight from the pot with a wooden spoon.
